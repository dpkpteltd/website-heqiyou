Safety, first — not last

A space that's safe enough to be yourself.

Anonymity is a promise.

Your identity stays yours until you both decide otherwise. We design for trust at every step, and you can leave anytime.

  • Verified phone, gated supply

    Every account starts with a verified phone number, and receivers pass quality gates before they can be matched.

  • Calls recorded & transcribed for safety

    You're told before every call. Audio is deleted right after transcription — the text exists so reports can actually be investigated.

  • Photos screened before display

    Every profile photo passes automated moderation before anyone sees it.

  • Report & block, one tap away

    Before, during, or after a call and in chat. Blocking ends contact instantly; reports get human review.

  • You can leave anytime

    Pass respectfully, end a call whenever you need to. No penalties, no guilt, no pressure to stay. Heqiyou is 18+ only.

Questions

Good to know.

During a call you're represented by a generative compatibility glyph — a shape, not a photo. No name, no face, no profile. Details are revealed only if you both choose to continue afterward.
Receiving voice dates is free, and you start with 60 welcome credits — three mini-dates on us. Optional rewarded ads add 20 credits each, up to five times a day. Credit packs (100 for US$4.99, 1,000 for US$29.99) exist only if you want more.
You choose a mood and a call length, and we gently pair you with someone online now who's looking for the same kind of conversation. No endless browsing — we bring the connection to you.
If you both choose to continue, your profiles unlock and a chat opens. If either of you passes, this connection ended here — quietly and respectfully, with no notification of who decided what.
Calls are recorded for safety so they can be transcribed for moderation. Once transcribed, the raw audio is deleted and only the text is retained — visible to the two of you and to safety reviewers, never to other users. You consent before every call, your number is never shared, and we don't touch your GPS or contacts. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
