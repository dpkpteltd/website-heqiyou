## 1. About the Service

1.1 Heqiyou is a voice-first social discovery and dating platform. Instead of browsing photos, users connect through anonymous, time-limited voice calls ("**Mini-Dates**"). Identities, names and photos are concealed during a Mini-Date. If — and only if — both participants choose to continue after the call, their profiles are mutually revealed and a private chat is unlocked.

1.2 The Service may include, without limitation: anonymous voice calling; queue-based matching; compatibility indicators; post-match messaging; direct (non-anonymous) voice calls between matched users; an AI-assisted date-planning feature; an AI voice companion; short-video posting ("**Glimpse**"); friend connections and an exclusive-commitment ("**Pledge**") feature; a shared "couple diary" of places ("**Places**"); profile creation and photo upload; in-app purchases of Credits; an auto-renewing **Premium** subscription; and rewarded advertising.

1.3 The Service is made available only in certain supported regions, as determined by us from time to time (each a "**Supported Region**"). Matching and Glimpse discovery are generally limited to users within the same Supported Region. We may add, remove, restrict or modify Supported Regions at any time without prior notice.

1.4 We are constantly improving the Service. We may add, change, suspend or discontinue any feature of the Service (in whole or in part), introduce new features (including features that are in development at the date of these Terms, such as enhanced identity verification or additional payment methods), or impose limits on features, at any time and at our sole discretion. Where a change materially and adversely affects paid features, we will use reasonable efforts to give you advance notice.

1.5 **Glimpse.** Glimpse lets you record a short video (about two seconds) of yourself and post it to a recurring time "window." Your clip is screened by automated moderation and then shown to a relevant audience: in the anonymous discovery view, to a compatibility-ranked group of other eligible users in your region for that window, shown with an abstract glyph and a compatibility score but not your name or profile (the video, however, shows your appearance); and, in the friends and pledged-partner views, with your name and photo to people you have connected with. Posting a Glimpse may consume Credits or be included with Premium (see Section 6).

1.6 **Connections, Friends and Pledge.** The Service lets you form ongoing connections with other users: a **Match** (after a mutual Mini-Date), a **Friend** connection (added using a personal friend code), and a **Pledge** — a mutual, exclusive commitment between matched users (formed using a match code). Pledging is exclusive: while pledged, certain blind-date and matching features are disabled, and some features are limited to your pledged partner. Connections can be ended at any time.

1.7 **Places (couple diary).** Connected users (matched or pledged) can keep a shared diary of places they have been together, including the place name and location, dates, photos, captions and notes contributed by either of you. Diary content is visible to both members of the connection. Posting a place may consume Credits or be included with Premium (see Section 6).

1.8 **AI voice companion.** The Service may offer a feature that lets you have a real-time voice conversation with an automated AI companion (not another user). It is time-limited and may consume Credits.
