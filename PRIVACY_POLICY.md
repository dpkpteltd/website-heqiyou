## 1. Summary (Read This First)

- Heqiyou is a voice-first dating service: you talk first, anonymously, and reveal profiles only by mutual choice. The design intentionally minimises what other users can see about you before you choose to be revealed.
- **Calls made through the Service are recorded and transcribed for safety.** Raw audio is deleted promptly after transcription; the text transcript is retained and is visible only to the call's participants and authorised personnel.
- **Some features let you share more of yourself.** "**Glimpse**" lets you post a short, self-recorded video (about two seconds). In the anonymous discovery view your name and profile stay hidden, but the **video itself shows your face and appearance**; with people you have connected to (friends or a pledged partner) your video is shown together with your name. The "**Places**" couple diary lets you and a connected partner record places you have been together, including the **location of those places**, photos, and notes.
- **Your conversations and content (call recordings, transcripts, chat messages, Glimpse videos and diary entries) may be used for the safety of users** — for example to investigate reports, detect abuse and enforce our rules — **and to enhance your own experience of the Service.**
- **Anonymized and aggregated data — data that can no longer reasonably identify you — may be used to improve and enhance the platform experience**, including matching quality, safety systems and product features.
- We do not sell your personal data.
- The Service is for adults aged 18 and over only.
- You can request access, correction or deletion of your personal data, and withdraw consent, by contacting **admin@deployku.com** or using in-app controls.

## 4. Voice, Video and Content Handling — How It Works

### 4.1 Call recording and transcription
Because voice is the core of the Service, we are specific about this:

1. **Disclosure and consent.** Before every call, the App reminds you that calls are recorded and that audio is transcribed for safety review. Joining the call constitutes consent.
2. **Recording.** Call audio is captured by our recording infrastructure and stored temporarily in secure cloud storage.
3. **Transcription.** The audio is transcribed by an automated speech-to-text service, then processed by a language-model service to label speakers and produce a short summary. These providers act on our behalf under contractual confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
4. **Deletion of audio.** **The raw audio recording is deleted promptly after transcription is complete.** We do not maintain a long-term archive of call audio.
5. **Retention of transcripts.** The text transcript, speaker labels and summary are retained and linked to the call.
6. **Access.** Transcripts are accessible only to (a) the two participants of that call, and (b) a limited number of authorised personnel, strictly for the safety, moderation, fraud-prevention, legal-compliance and experience purposes described in Section 3. Transcripts are never shown to other users.

### 4.2 Glimpse video clips
1. **What it is.** Glimpse lets you record a short video (about two seconds) of yourself and post it to a recurring time "window." The clip shows your face/appearance.
2. **Moderation.** Before a clip is shown to anyone, it is screened by an automated content-moderation provider for prohibited content (for example, nudity, weapons, drugs or offensive material). Clips that fail are deleted and not shown.
3. **Who can see it.** In the **anonymous ("Hopeful") view**, your clip is shown to a small, compatibility-ranked group of other eligible users in your region for that window, **alongside an abstract glyph and a compatibility score but not your name or profile** — though the video necessarily shows your appearance. In the **Friends** and **partner ("You & …")** views, your clip is shown together with your name and photo to people you have connected with.
4. **Storage and delivery.** Approved clips are stored with a specialist object-storage provider and delivered to viewers through a content-delivery network using short-lived, signed links, so a clip cannot be accessed without a valid, time-limited link issued by us.
5. **Lifecycle.** Clips are tied to their time window; older windows stop being surfaced in discovery. Clips are deleted when you delete your account (see Section 8), and you can delete your own clips in-app.

### 4.3 Places couple diary
Entries you create in the Places couple diary — place names, locations, dates, photos, captions and notes — are stored in private storage and are visible to both members of that connection. Media is delivered through signed links. Because a diary is shared, an entry or contribution you add can be seen (and retained) by the connected partner.

### 4.4 AI voice companion ("Resonate")
If you use the AI voice companion, your microphone audio is streamed to a conversational-AI voice provider acting on our behalf so the AI can understand you and respond in real time. This is a conversation with an automated agent, not another user.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, to comply with legal obligations, to resolve disputes, and to enforce agreements. Indicative retention practices:

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Raw call audio | **Deleted promptly after transcription** (typically immediately upon successful processing) |
| Call transcripts, labels, summaries | Retained while your account is active; deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period after account deletion, except where retention is required for safety investigations, disputes or legal obligations |
| Chat messages | Retained while your account is active; removed from the Service upon account deletion (the other participant's copy of a conversation may persist in de-identified form) |
| Glimpse video clips | Retained while your account is active; older windows stop being surfaced in discovery; clips that fail moderation are deleted immediately; all your clips are deleted upon account deletion |
| Places diary entries, photos and notes | Retained while the connection and your account are active; deleted upon account deletion (a connected partner's copy of the shared diary may persist for that partner) |
| Profile data and photos | Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account deletion. Photos rejected by moderation are deleted |
| Connection/relationship records (friends, matches, pledges) | Retained while active; removed or de-identified on account deletion, subject to safety/legal retention |
| One-time login codes | Minutes (expire shortly after issuance) |
| Credit/transaction ledger; subscription/entitlement records | Retained for the life of the account and thereafter as required for accounting, tax, audit, fraud-prevention and legal purposes (the ledger is append-only by design) |
| Reports, blocks, ratings, enforcement records | Retained as long as necessary for safety and legal purposes, including after account deletion where needed to prevent banned users from returning or to address ongoing investigations |
| Device tokens and technical logs | Short, rolling retention periods appropriate to their diagnostic and security purpose |
| Anonymized/aggregated data | May be retained indefinitely (no longer personal data) |

When retention ends, we delete or irreversibly anonymize the data. Residual copies may persist for a limited time in encrypted backups before being overwritten in the ordinary course.

## 10. Security

We implement administrative, technical and organisational measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data we hold, including:

- encryption of data in transit;
- database-level access rules that restrict each user's access to their own records (for example, transcripts readable only by call participants, and a couple diary readable only by its two members);
- delivery of stored media (Glimpse videos and diary photos) through short-lived, signed links rather than public URLs;
- security credentials and call/session tokens issued only server-side, never embedded in the App;
- an append-only financial ledger that cannot be retroactively edited;
- least-privilege access controls, secrets management, and logging for our personnel and systems;
- automated screening of uploaded photos and videos before display;
- vendor due-diligence and contractual data-protection obligations for all service providers.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a data breach occurs that is notifiable under applicable law, we will notify the relevant authority and affected users as required.
